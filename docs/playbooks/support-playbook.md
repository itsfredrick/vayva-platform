# Support Playbook

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025  
**Purpose:** Guide support staff behavior without improvisation

---

## Support Tier Structure

### Tier 1: General Support

**Handles:** Basic questions, feature clarification, billing

### Tier 2: Operational Issues

**Handles:** Missing orders, payment mismatches, delivery disputes

### Tier 3: Trust & Safety

**Handles:** Fraud reports, prohibited items, KYC issues

---

## Tier 1: General Support

### Scope

**Handles:**

- "How do I...?" questions
- Feature explanations
- Account setup help
- Billing questions
- Password resets
- General navigation

**Response Time:** 24 hours (business days)

### Rules

#### ✅ Do:

- Always reference Help Center first
- Use templates for common questions
- Be patient and friendly
- Document all interactions
- Escalate when uncertain

#### ❌ Never:

- Give legal advice
- Promise refunds outside policy
- Make commitments about features
- Share other users' information
- Bypass security procedures

### Common Scenarios

#### "How do I record a payment?"

**Response:**

> To record a payment:
>
> 1. Go to Orders
> 2. Select the order
> 3. Click "Record Payment"
> 4. Enter amount and method
>
> For detailed steps, see: [Help Center Link]

#### "Can I get a refund?"

**Response:**

> Our refund policy is available at [link].
>
> Generally:
>
> - Free plans: No charges, no refunds needed
> - Paid plans: Refunds handled per policy
>
> Would you like me to review your specific situation?

#### "Why was I charged?"

**Response:**

> Let me check your billing history.
>
> [After checking]
> You were charged ₦[amount] for [plan name] on [date].
> This is a monthly subscription charge.
>
> Would you like to review or cancel your subscription?

### Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Tier 2 if:

- User reports missing data
- Billing discrepancy
- Technical error
- Feature not working as expected

## Tier 2: Operational Issues

### Scope

**Handles:**

- Missing orders or payments
- Data sync issues
- Payment mismatches
- Delivery coordination problems
- Export/report errors

**Response Time:** 4 hours (business hours)

### Rules

#### ✅ Do:

- Investigate thoroughly before responding
- Check logs and system status
- Document findings
- Provide clear explanations
- Offer workarounds if available

#### ❌ Never:

- Blame the user
- Make manual data edits without approval
- Promise specific timelines for fixes
- Dismiss user concerns
- Skip documentation

### Common Scenarios

#### "My orders are missing"

**Investigation Checklist:**

1. Check user's WhatsApp connection status
2. Review sync logs for errors
3. Verify orders exist in database
4. Check for filter/search issues

**Response Template:**

> I've investigated your missing orders issue.
>
> [If sync issue]
> I found that your WhatsApp connection was interrupted on [date].
> Your orders are safe and will sync once reconnected.
>
> To reconnect: [steps]
>
> [If user error]
> I found your orders under [location/filter].
> They weren't missing, just filtered out.
>
> [If actual bug]
> I've identified a technical issue affecting your account.
> Our engineering team is investigating.
> Expected resolution: [timeframe if known]

#### "Payment amounts don't match"

**Investigation Checklist:**

1. Compare order total vs. payment recorded
2. Check for partial payments
3. Review payment method
4. Verify currency/exchange rates if applicable

**Response Template:**

> I've reviewed your payment records.
>
> Order #[ID]: ₦[amount]
> Payment recorded: ₦[amount]
>
> [If mismatch explained]
> The difference is due to [reason].
>
> [If error]
> This appears to be a data entry error.
> You can edit the payment record to correct it.
>
> [If system issue]
> I've flagged this for our engineering team.

### Escalation Triggers

Escalate to Tier 3 if:

- Data integrity risk
- Suspected fraud
- Prohibited items involved
- Legal/compliance concern
- Requires manual database intervention

## Tier 3: Trust & Safety

### Scope

**Handles:**

- Fraud reports
- Prohibited items
- KYC verification issues
- Account suspension appeals
- Legal/compliance matters

**Response Time:** 2 hours (critical), 8 hours (standard)

### Rules

#### ✅ Do:

- Document everything meticulously
- Use neutral, professional language
- Follow legal/compliance procedures
- Consult legal team when uncertain
- Preserve evidence
- Maintain confidentiality

#### ❌ Never:

- Accuse users without evidence
- Share investigation details publicly
- Make threats
- Use moral/judgmental language
- Negotiate on legal violations
- Skip documentation

### Common Scenarios

#### "I want to report fraud"

**Investigation Protocol:**

1. Gather details from reporter
2. Review reported account/activity
3. Check for policy violations
4. Document evidence
5. Escalate to legal if needed

**Response Template:**

> Thank you for reporting this concern.
>
> We take fraud seriously and will investigate.
>
> What I need from you:
>
> - [Specific details]
> - [Evidence if available]
> - [Your contact info for follow-up]
>
> Our investigation timeline: [timeframe]
>
> We cannot share investigation outcomes due to privacy,
> but we will take appropriate action if violations are confirmed.

#### "Why was my account suspended?"

**Response Protocol:**

1. Review suspension reason in system
2. Verify policy violation
3. Check for appeals history
4. Prepare clear explanation

**Response Template:**

> Your account was suspended on [date] for: [reason]
>
> This violates our [policy name]: [link]
>
> Specifically: [clear explanation without accusatory language]
>
> To appeal:
>
> 1. [Steps]
> 2. [Required information]
>
> Appeals are reviewed within [timeframe].

#### "I need KYC verification help"

**Response Template:**

> To verify your account, we need:
>
> 1. Government-issued ID (NIN, Driver's License, or Passport)
> 2. Business registration (if applicable)
> 3. Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
>
> How to submit:
> [Upload instructions]
>
> Review timeline: 2-3 business days
>
> Why we need this: [brief, non-threatening explanation]

### Prohibited Items Response

**If user asks about selling prohibited item:**

**Response:**

> [Item] is prohibited on Vayva per our Prohibited Items Policy: [link]
>
> We cannot support sales of this item.
>
> If you believe this is an error, please contact: legal@vayva.shop

**If prohibited item detected:**

**Action:**

1. Document evidence
2. Remove listing
3. Notify user
4. Escalate to legal if severe

**Notification Template:**

> We've removed your listing for [item] as it violates our Prohibited Items Policy.
>
> Policy: [link]
>
> Repeated violations may result in account suspension.
>
> If you have questions: legal@vayva.shop

## Communication Rules (All Tiers)

### Language Guidelines

#### ✅ Use:

- Clear, simple language
- Professional tone
- Empathy and patience
- Specific details
- Policy references

#### ❌ Avoid:

- Jargon or technical terms
- Blame or accusation
- Promises we can't keep
- Moral judgments
- Sarcasm or humor (in serious matters)

### Templates

All responses should:

1. Acknowledge the issue
2. Explain what we found
3. Provide next steps
4. Set expectations
5. Offer help

### Escalation Path

```
Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3 → Legal/Engineering
```

**When to escalate:**

- Outside your scope
- Requires technical intervention
- Legal/compliance concern
- User requests escalation
- Uncertain about response
