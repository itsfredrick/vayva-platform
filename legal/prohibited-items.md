# Prohibited Items Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

---

## Purpose

This policy outlines products and services that **may not be sold** using the Vayva platform, in compliance with Nigerian law and platform integrity requirements.

---

## Prohibited Items

You may not use Vayva to sell:

### 1. Illegal Substances
- Narcotics or controlled substances
- Illegal drugs or drug paraphernalia
- Prescription medications without proper licensing

### 2. Weapons and Ammunition
- Firearms, guns, or ammunition
- Explosives or fireworks
- Knives or bladed weapons (except kitchen utensils)
- Tasers, pepper spray, or similar items

### 3. Counterfeit or Stolen Goods
- Counterfeit products or replicas
- Stolen property
- Goods infringing intellectual property rights
- Unauthorized branded merchandise

### 4. Fraudulent Financial Instruments
- Counterfeit currency
- Fake documents or IDs
- Pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing
- Unauthorized financial services

### 5. Adult Content
- Pornographic materials
- Adult services
- Items primarily intended for sexual use

### 6. Hazardous Materials
- Toxic or poisonous substances
- Radioactive materials
- Biohazardous waste

### 7. Regulated Items Without Proper Licensing
- Tobacco products (without license)
- Alcoholic beverages (without license)
- Pharmaceuticals (without license)
- Medical devices (without certification)

### 8. Items Illegal Under Nigerian Law
- Any product or service prohibited by:
  - Federal laws of Nigeria
  - State or local regulations
  - Industry-specific regulations

---

## Enforcement

### Violation Consequences
- Immediate listing removal
- Account suspension or termination
- Reporting to relevant authorities
- Legal action where applicable

### Reporting
If you encounter prohibited items on Vayva, report to:  
**Email:** compliance@vayva.shop

---

## Disclaimer

This list is not exhaustive. Vayva reserves the right to prohibit additional items at our discretion.

Merchants are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with all applicable laws.
